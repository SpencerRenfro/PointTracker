How to Use These QR Codes

  1. Start the Point Tracker app on your device
  2. On the home screen, tap "Start Tour" for the Campus Tour
  3. Navigate to the Scan tab
  4. Point your camera at each QR code to scan it
  5. After scanning, you'll see information about that location
  6. After scanning all four points, you'll see the tour completion screen

Tip: You can print this page to have physical QR codes for testing.
